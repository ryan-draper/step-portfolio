Projects

Disease Transmission Mapping (Python)

March 2020

  • Implemented functions to read in disease transmission data and build a graph where nodes represent hospital patients and edges represent the likelihood of connectedness in transmission based on time difference between hospital visits and gene similarity
  • Computed the rooted directed minimum spanning tree (RDMST) of the graph to determine the most likely transmission map, which involves functions such as computing cycles in the graph, contracting cycles into single nodes, and expanding the contracted cycles

Houston Crime Prediction (Python, pandas, scikit-learn)
